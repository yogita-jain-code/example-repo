QMS (Unplanned Mode)

Step-1 Generate Id & Take Photos

Fill in the registration form and click on Submit button. After the registration process, login by entering the registered mobile number. On successful verification the home page is displayed with three options:
1.Add Feedback
2. View & Upload Feedback
3. Profile

Step-2 Finalize

To post a feedback click on Add Feedback button which provides option to:
1. Capture/Select Photograph
   a) Take Photograph using App
   b) Choose from Gallery
      (Geotagged photos only)
 Kindly ensure the GPS is ON mode before capturing the photographs
2. After selecting the required photographs click on Proceed to Feedback
3.Enter the required details of the feedback and click on Save Feedback button.

Step-3 Assign Road & Fill Observation

Click on View & Upload Feedback button to view the list of feedbacks posted with an option to:
1. View & Submit (To upload the feedback if all the entered details are correct)
2. Update (To update the required details of the feedback)
3. Delete (To delete the feedback)

Step-4 Submit Observation

Click on Get Status button to view the latest status of the submitted feedback from the concerned authorities.
